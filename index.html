Favsales

favsales is an email invoice and payment system for Facebook page merchants. This app allows the merchants to generate email invoices on Facebook comments and accept credit card payments. The concept is very much on the lines of Soldsie.com.

3Mik

3Mik is the repository that powers our website www.3mik.com. 3mik is a shopping curation site and a social network.

webgloo micro mvc framework

A small modern day PHP 5.3+ library that provides
